Tofu and Fresh Vegetable Soup

This nourishing Cantonese Soup is packed with vegetables. You can vary them as you like, but make sure to have contrasting colors, textures, and flavorsp>

The ingrediants that you will need are

  1. 2-3 ounces each carrots, drained sanned sliced bamboo shoots, button or field mushrooms,asparagus, and bean sprouts. ONE cake firm tofu, 2 and a half cups of Chinese Stock, ONE tablespoon of light soy sauce, Salt and Pepper, a few drops of sesame oil
  2. Tea
  3. Milk
