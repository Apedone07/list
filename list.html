Tofu and Fresh Vegetable Soup

This nourishing Cantonese Soup is packed with vegetables. You can vary them as you like, but make sure to have contrasting colors, textures, and flavorsp>

The ingrediants that you will need are

  1. 2-3 ounces each carrots, drained sanned sliced bamboo shoots, button or field mushrooms,asparagus, and bean sprouts. ONE cake firm tofu, 2 and a half cups of Chinese Stock, ONE tablespoon of light soy sauce, Salt and Pepper, a few drops of sesame oil
  2. step one - cut all the vegetables into thin slices or shreds. Cut the tofu into thin slices lengthwise then cut again lengthwise into strips and crosswise into cubes.
  3. step two - bring the stock to a rolling boil in a wok, add the carrot snd tofu and bring back to a boil. Cook for one minute then add the bamboo shoots and asparagus and cook for another minute.
  4. step three - add the mushrooms and bean sprouts and cook for another minute. Add the soy sauce and salt and pepper, add sesame oil for taste. Serve hot.
